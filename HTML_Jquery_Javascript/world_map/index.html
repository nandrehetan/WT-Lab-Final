Maps of the World and India

World Map

The world map is a map of most or all of the surface of the Earth. World maps, because of their scale, must deal with the problem of projection. Maps rendered in two dimensions by necessity distort the display of the three-dimensional surface of the earth.

Map of India

India is located in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world. The map of India shows its 28 states and 8 union territories, each with distinct cultures and languages.
